# Basic Features

## Messaging

### Private Chats
- Send text messages
- Share photos and videos
- Record voice messages
- Share files and documents
- Use emojis and stickers

### Group Chats
- Create groups up to 200,000 members
- Add admins and moderators
- Pin important messages
- Share media and files

## Media Sharing

### Photos & Videos
- Send high-quality photos
- Share videos up to 2GB
- Edit photos before sending
- Send self-destructing media

### Files & Documents
- Share files up to 2GB
- Preview documents
- Organize with folders
- Cloud storage

## Voice & Video

### Voice Messages
- Record voice messages
- Play at 2x speed
- Waveform visualization

### Voice & Video Calls
- Make one-on-one calls
- Group voice chats
- Video calls
- Screen sharing
